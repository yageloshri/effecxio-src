Our Story

From Bean to Cup

Ritual started in 2019 with a simple belief: great coffee is a daily ritual worth celebrating. We source beans directly from farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala.

Every cup is roasted in-house, ground to order, and prepared by baristas who care. Our kitchen follows the same philosophy — seasonal, local, and made fresh every morning.
