You get full access to all Pro features for 14 days. No credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can upgrade or continue with the free plan.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

Teams of 10+ get 20% off. Teams of 50+ get 30% off. Contact sales for custom enterprise pricing.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, use end-to-end encryption, and your data is stored in ISO 27001 certified data centers.
