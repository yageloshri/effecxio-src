Governance Infrastructure

On-Chain Voting

Every vote is recorded on-chain. Fully transparent, tamper-proof, and verifiable.

Delegation

Delegate your voting power to trusted community members without giving up your tokens.

Timelock Execution

Passed proposals execute after a 48h timelock, giving the community time to review.

Multi-Sig Treasury

Treasury controlled by 5/9 multi-sig with on-chain spending transparency.

Snapshot Integration

Gasless off-chain polling for temperature checks before on-chain votes.

Custom Quorum

Set flexible quorum requirements per proposal type. Emergency vs standard governance.
