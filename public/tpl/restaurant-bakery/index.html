Our story

What started as a small home kitchen experiment has grown into Tel Aviv's beloved neighborhood bakery. We still do everything by hand — from mixing dough at dawn to pulling the last loaf at dusk.

We believe great bread takes time. That's why every product in our shop follows slow fermentation methods perfected over a decade.
